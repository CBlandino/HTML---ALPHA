About ALPHA

Welcome to ALPHA, your destination for affordable and high-quality clothing that stands the test of time. Here's a little bit about us:

Our Mission

Our mission at ALPHA is to provide easily identifiable clothing made from sustainable materials. We believe in offering affordable options without compromising on quality.

Our Commitment to Quality

Unlike many brands that prioritize cost-cutting, we prioritize quality and sustainability. Our clothing is made from organic cotton, hemp, linen, tencel, and peace silk, making them more breathable, softer, and environmentally friendly.

Who We Are

We are a group of college students who are passionate about raising the standard for clothing. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a brand that offers reliable clothing made from sustainable materials.
